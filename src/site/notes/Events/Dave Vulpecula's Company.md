[[Related Characters/Dave Vulpecula\|Dave Vulpecula]]'s Company was under Gensai Industries, they created mechs originally and then moved onto robots due to an incident where an employee was baked alive inside and was not able to get out of the mech. This was at the very start of the company and eventually would be covered up in contract with Gensai.

This led to robots being created, combat robots originally. They were a hit for their resistance and agility at the same time and were a feat of their time. Things would have been fine if the cover up was not investigated, which eventually led to [[Related Characters/Dave Vulpecula\|Dave Vulpecula]] to take fault for the incident and the shutdown of the company. 

Later on, before [[OCs/Orion\|Orion]] was made, he would steal parts from the company's abandoned warehouses to create Orion after the loss of his son. Eventually Orion was made and they ran a shady black market for awhile until it began to crumble as well.
