When [[OCs/Glam\|Glam]], as a watcher of universes/potential ones or basically timelines created by single decisions. A lower hellish form of 'void' would merge with one of his universes causing others to deteriorate around that one. 

To prevent this he would go into one of these universes that had not yet been 'infected'. This would cause a ripple effect that would 'rip out that void' from the others reversing the effects it caused. 

This would make part of Glam split into *other realities* [[Dimensions/Timelines\|Timelines]] and he would never be the same again as a watcher.
