A realm [[OCs/Glam\|Glam]] uses to teleport/warp to others, he also brings others in here but there are others that are able to travel within it too

This realm is purely white with some possible landmarks in the distance but they are too far to tell. The ground seems to be grassy and it is possible to manifest things here with concentration. Its very silent here but there seems to be no echo within
