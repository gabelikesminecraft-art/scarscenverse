Dave Vulpecula also nicknamed "The Fox" was [[OCs/Orion\|Orion]]'s creator, he owned a company under Gensai Industries and when that failed he turned to crime. 

He had a wife and a son, later on at the end of his company's life([[Events/Dave Vulpecula's Company\|Dave Vulpecula's Company]]) he got a divorce, and before that his son would have died. 

When his black market was found out he was killed by Orion in a swat raid. During that time he forced Orion to do his bidding.
