NAME: Glam Glorious Glamour 
AGE: Possibly Hundreds of Thousands or more (August 26th)
GENDER: He/Him, Male
SPECIES: [[Species/Divine\|Divine]] (9th dimensional)
HEIGHT: 5' 8''

Glam is, or what he thinks he is, a lost king and a thief who was forced out of his kingdom by a destructive force. He thinks he had stolen a god's heart and merged with it which is partially true in some ways.

In reality, Glam was originally a watcher of certain streamlines of time which would create other universes, and would keep them in check for any reality benders/reality destroyers. Eventually a Void parasite (not related to the voidstar), would take over a reality and infect others around it which would create a paradox where Glam existed by not existing so part of him would split off into an untouched reality. all of this is in [[Events/Glam's Creation\| in a more detailed fashion at least.]] 

Glam can really do about anything he wants, but is limited when in his own realities. There are actually two glams in the ones he's in, since he is connected to them meaning some part of the real Glam is still in other dimensions

This event would send Glam into insanity for awhile before he was somewhat stable, but even still he has these thoughts he has never really experienced
