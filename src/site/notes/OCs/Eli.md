NAME: Eli Jay Tilton
AGE: 29 (July 7th)
GENDER: He/Him
SPECIES: Human
HEIGHT: 5' 9''


Eli Jay Tilton is more of an average joe, inspired/taken directly from Monument Mythos' "The House In the Ocean" (although I have tried to make it more original). 

[[Dimensions/Starfall\|Starfall]] : In Starfall he is a Astronomist (Mainly) and Xenotheoretical Biologist. This story also applies to pine ridge in some way for his backstory. His backstory is most just of his normal life, being known as his towns astronomist in Gainesville Florida. One night his friend send him some cords to the middle of the lake with a photo of 5 blurry celestial objects in the sky unlike seen before. When he took a boat out he presumed his friend went home, but would inspect through a telescope that the celestial objects were in fact not stars, nothing likely to a meteor or comet either. The last thing he remembers of that night were those stars approaching him and then seeing they were almost starfish like, with tentacles wiggling out to their sides. He was knocked into the water and sunk down seeing his friend([[Related Characters/Related Characters\|Related Characters]]) along with the boat at the bottom of the lake. And then passing out as he awoke on the shore, and then transported into a hospital

[[Dimensions/Pine Ridge\|Pine Ridge]]: The same story is happening currently akin to Starfall. In  Pine ridge he works at blockbuster and is a criminal with Xemmie. To Xemmie he is his 'Day two'
