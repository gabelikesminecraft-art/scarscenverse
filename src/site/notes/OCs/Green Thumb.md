NAME: Gary Glarson, A.k.a "The Green Thumb"
AGE: 42 (April 17th)
GENDER: He/Him Male
SPECIES: [[Species/Thumbling(s)\|Thumbling(s)]] (mainly)
HEIGHT:6' 1'' (usually)


The Green Thumb, or Gary Glarson, is a [[Species/Thumbling(s)\|Thumbling(s)]] from the planet thumb. He ran away from a tyranny and lost a couple friends on the way. He landed on earth in a starship into Neon District (Originally, [[Dimensions/Timelines\|Timelines]]). Eventually he would be kidnapped into a facility to be studied after wandering the streets for awhile. When he blew up and escaped that facility and learned most of his friends died he wanted a way out and would contact [[OCs/Glingus\|Glingus]], who was a traveler passing through. When making The Green Thumb leave something would happen and split him into multiple places at once making him into an ooze. 

The green thumb has stretchy powers and is considered 'Superhuman' to some capacity.

[[Dimensions/Starfall\|Starfall]]: He was able to control his oozy self and contort himself to be able to wander and wiggle through the walls. 

[[Dimensions/Pine Ridge\|Pine Ridge]]: This part of The Green Thumb was split into 3 universes making him fully into a true oozy form known as "The Green Thing" By locals.

Neon District: During his times here he would sneak around official buildings and whatnot as well.
