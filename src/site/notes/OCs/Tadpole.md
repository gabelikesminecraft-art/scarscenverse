NAME: Tadpole Smoggy Jenkins
AGE: 26 (February 26th)
GENDER: He/Him Male
SPECIES: [[Species/Humanoid\|Humanoid]]
HEIGHT: 5' 6.5''

Tadpole is a frog person who likes baked goods and is partially a scientist as well. He uses potions to make life flourish. He has a brother named [[Related Characters/Goldtoad\|Goldtoad]] which is almost just the pure opposite of Tadpole.

[[Dimensions/Starfall\|Starfall]](Briefly): He was a botanist on the ship, and would leave right before the ORIGINAL ship was destroyed by Petra guardians. He would learn about it later on during his vacation and wouldn't come back

[[Dimensions/Pine Ridge\|Pine Ridge]]: Rana (AU) is a baker at a cafe, who doesn't exactly mind all the stuff happening. Friends with Jabarri.

[[Dimensions/Minecraft\|Minecraft]]: Owner of Flower Town, eventually he would put himself in a mossy cocoon and merge with the land, watching from a distance. He would come back after rebuilding himself with his original look, being made of tiny flowers.
