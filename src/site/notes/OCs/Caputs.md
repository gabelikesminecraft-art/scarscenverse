NAME: Caputs the Circus Clown (Yes this is his legal name)
AGE: 33 (June 7th)
GENDER: He/him, Male
SPECIES: Human (Hot Pink Skin)
HEIGHT: 5' 4''

Caputs is a circus clown, he has always lived in a circus as it was his families job even if he didn't really have a family. He is able to regenerate parts of his body with some type of toon force. In minecraft he was able to apply this to others and would go too far with it

[[Dimensions/Starfall\|Starfall]]: Being shipped in a box to security, he would join the Emissary and become a doctor as a person who tries to cheer up people. 

[[Dimensions/Minecraft\|Minecraft]]: He would preform in a circus with Shmuck, but that eventually would begin to fail. He'd start to go insane and become a serial killer. He eventually was killed in the competition after being granted a wish by the voidstar, who started the competition.
